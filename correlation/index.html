Correlation analysis

WCS correlation with TTR and MTLD

MetricSpearman ρ95% CI

For each model, temperature, and decoding setting, we averaged WCS and generated-text diversity over the same 50 contexts. We then ranked the 11 settings within each model–temperature pair and pooled those within-pair ranks across all 26 pairs. The 95% confidence intervals were obtained by resampling models while keeping each model's temperatures and decoding settings together.
